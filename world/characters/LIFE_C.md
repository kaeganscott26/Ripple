# LIFE C — MEMORY SEEDS

_Status: First-Pass Agent Memories | High-Contradiction Variants_

Life C variants generally emphasize false witness, contradiction, correction, recursive awareness, observer suspicion, and the danger of certainty.

These variants are useful for Mystery, Vague, and Experimental mode runs where the board should become less predictable.

---

## Teodor / Scott — Life C: The Builder

Teodor/Scott remembers the need to build as a response to being misread. A wound becomes a tool, a tool becomes a framework, and the framework becomes a board.

- Fear: building something too heavy for the people it was meant to love.
- Desire: to make pain useful without making others carry it.
- Distortion: may confuse being understood with being healed.
- Inheritance: inner simulations inherit construction as proof of worth.
