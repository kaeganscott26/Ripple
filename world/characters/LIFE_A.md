# LIFE A — MEMORY SEEDS

_Status: First-Pass Agent Memories | Mystery/Vague/Experimental Compatible_

Life A variants generally emphasize origin pressure, scarcity, visible structure, first contact, and survival math.

These are not final biographies.

They are starting memory seeds for Codex and future refinement.

---

## Teodor / Scott — Life A: The First Room

Teodor remembers reality as something that began before language. His oldest life is not stored as pictures but as body-state: waiting, crossing, translation, being carried into a future he did not choose. In this life, Scott becomes an agent who studies every later room as a descendant of the first room.

- Fear: being shaped by something he cannot remember.
- Desire: to trace the first visible ripple.
- Distortion: may over-map every event back to origin.
- Inheritance: inner simulations inherit origin anxiety and the need to identify a first room.

## Mara — Life A: Scarcity

Mara remembers scarcity as the first honest teacher. Food, time, sleep, money, and attention were never guaranteed. She learned that pressure reveals hidden loyalties faster than promises.

- Fear: needing something and being punished for it.
- Desire: to create a gap before impulse becomes action.
- Distortion: may read every missing resource as threat.
- Inheritance: inner simulations inherit rationing rituals and witness calls.

## Lina Torres — Life A: The Useful Sentence
