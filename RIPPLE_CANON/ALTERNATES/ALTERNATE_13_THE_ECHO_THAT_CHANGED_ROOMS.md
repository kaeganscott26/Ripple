## 📖 Alternate Story

The signal leaves the first room and comes back changed.

At first, [[Teodor / Scott]] thinks the change means distortion.

Then the board shows a different possibility.

An echo can carry new information because another room touched it.

[[Mara]] reads the echo as witness expansion. More than one room can confirm that something mattered.

[[Jamal]] reads the echo as form migration. A phrase moves from private language into shared handle.

[[Maren]] reads the echo as mood transfer. The way people receive the signal changes the emotional weather around it.

[[Dev]] reads the echo as access through another route. The first door was not the only path.

[[Teodor / Scott]] reads the echo as proof that a theory is not alive until another room can speak it back in its own language.

The echo returns, but it is not obedience.

It is translation.
