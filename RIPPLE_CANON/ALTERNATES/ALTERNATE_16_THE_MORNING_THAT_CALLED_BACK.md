## 📖 Alternate Story

The morning arrives with the same light, but not the same silence.

The signal from the night before does not vanish into sleep, static, or memory. It leaves a trace strong enough for the next room to answer.

[[Mara]] reads the morning as witness return. The room can finally compare what was sent with what remained.

[[Jamal]] reads the morning as form update. The signal has become record, but the record is not allowed to become a cage.

[[Maren]] reads the morning as mood evidence. The body of the room remembers what the mind tried to sort later.

[[Dev]] reads the morning as access delayed but not lost. The door did not open in the night, but the path did not disappear.

[[Teodor / Scott]] reads the morning as the difference between echo and return. An echo repeats. A return changes what can happen next.
