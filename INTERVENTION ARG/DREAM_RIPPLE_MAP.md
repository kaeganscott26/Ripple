Dreams create simulated rooms.

Those rooms may contain:

- familiar houses,
- distorted parents,
- impossible hallways,
- childhood fear,
- dead voices,
- future anxiety,
- relapse echoes,
- doors that should not open,
- rooms that feel remembered but never existed.

The mind renders the room.

The body believes it long enough to react.

The waking self inherits the emotional weather.
