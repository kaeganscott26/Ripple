# ⚠️ Boundaries

INTERVENTION is fiction.

The repo is designed to feel like a found artifact, but the boundary matters.

---

## Read This First

INTERVENTION intentionally blurs fiction, reflection, and real-world noticing.

That blur is part of the art.

It is also why this boundary exists.

Your life is not the ARG.

Reality is reality.

The ARG is a fictionalized lens.

Noticing a pattern is not the same as receiving a command.

Coincidence is allowed to remain coincidence.

A symbol is allowed to remain a symbol.

A room is allowed to just be a room.

For a fuller warning, read [Mental Health Disclaimer](MENTAL_HEALTH_DISCLAIMER.md).

---

## What This Is Not

- This is not therapy.

- This is not crisis support.

- This is not proof that fiction is secretly real.
- This is not permission to diagnose other people.

If you are unsafe, contact a trusted real person, local emergency support, or a crisis line in the world you physically inhabit.

If you are in the United States and in crisis, you can call or text 988.

---

## Mental Health Boundary

This project is not recommended for anyone currently struggling to separate fiction from reality, experiencing paranoia, mania, psychosis, delusions, dissociation, severe crisis, or any mental state where pattern-based fiction could make symptoms worse.

If reading this project makes you feel unsafe, watched, commanded, chosen, targeted, or unable to tell where the story ends and your physical life begins, stop reading and contact a trusted real person or local professional support.

## Rules of the Room

No story outranks the body.

No metaphor outranks safety.

No framework outranks consent.

No ripple excuses harm.

Do not build cages and call them worlds.

Do not turn crisis into gameplay.

---

## Community Participation

Issues, Discussions, and artifact submissions are welcome only if they keep the room safe.

Before posting:

- Remove private information.
- Remove names of people who did not consent.
- Remove addresses, phone numbers, workplaces, and private messages.
- Do not diagnose strangers.
- Do not make real people into characters without consent.
- Do not turn crisis into gameplay.
- Do not use the repo as a replacement for support in the physical world.

The goal is not to prove the story is real.

The goal is to notice what becomes visible after reading it.
