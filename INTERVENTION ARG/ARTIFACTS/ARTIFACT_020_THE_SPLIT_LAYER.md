## 🪞 Field Note

A split is not always a dramatic break.

Sometimes it is one choice made quietly.

A message sent.

A message withheld.

A door opened.

A door avoided.

A person leaving the room before the room finishes teaching them who they are allowed to become.

The Split Layer studies the moment where one life branches into two possible rooms.

Not destiny.

Not prophecy.

A fork.

A consequence.

A path that becomes easier to repeat because it has already been walked once.

---

## 🧭 Ripple Theory Connection

Ripple Theory needs the Split Layer because intervention points do not always look like rescue.

Sometimes they look like delay.

Sometimes they look like silence.

Sometimes they look like a person choosing the smaller harm because the larger room is not survivable yet.

```text
pressure
      ↓
choice point
      ↓
path A / path B
      ↓
repeated consequence
      ↓
identity adapts
      ↓
future rooms change
```

The split is where the ripple becomes visible as direction.

## 👪 Family Current

Families create split layers constantly.

Who stays.

Who leaves.

Who apologizes.

Who cannot.

Who becomes the warning.

Who becomes the proof that the warning can be survived.

A family can become a room with old scripts already written into the walls.

An intervention point is the moment someone notices the script before handing it to the next person.
