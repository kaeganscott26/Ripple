# 🗃️ Official Artifacts

This folder contains the official artifact layer for INTERVENTION.

Artifacts are not side quests.

They are field notes, theory fragments, recovered signals, memory documents, safety handrails, and real-world echoes that help the reader understand Ripple Theory without turning the theory into an unsafe claim.

Some artifacts are strange.

Some are sad.

Some are funny.

Some are technical.

Some are historical.

That is the point.

The room is not always dramatic when it changes.

---

## 🗝️ Canon Boundary

This is the official artifact layer.

Artifacts in this folder are curated parts of INTERVENTION.

Reader submissions do not belong here by default.

Community responses, reader field notes, and outside echoes belong in `COMMUNITY/` unless the curator intentionally moves them into the official layer.

Readers can add echoes.

Only the curator decides what becomes part of the room.

---

## 🧱 Official Artifact Format

Every artifact should use this structure unless the artifact is intentionally preserved as a long-form narrative document.

```text
# ARTIFACT_OR_FIELDNOTE_TITLE

_Status: Layer | Current | Guardrail | Reality Remains Reality_

---

## [emoji] Field Note / Core Thesis / Artifact Body

[artifact text]

---

## 🧭 Ripple Theory Connection

[how this supports, tests, challenges, or clarifies the theory]

---

## ⚠️ Guardrail

[what this artifact must not be used to prove or excuse]

---

## 🔗 Obsidian Links

- [[LAYER_OR_CHAPTER]] — reason

---

## 🔥 Key Lines

> [strongest lines]

Fine.

<!--
[ARTIFACT LOG]: short description
-->
```
