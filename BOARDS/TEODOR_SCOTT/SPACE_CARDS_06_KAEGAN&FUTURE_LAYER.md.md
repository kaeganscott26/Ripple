# TEODOR / SCOTT SPACE CARDS

## Batch 06 — Kaegan / Future Layer

_Status: Board Space Cards | Spaces 41–48 | Kaegan / Future Layer Zone | AI Ending Fuel_

---

## Zone Function

The Kaegan / Future Layer zone changes what consequence means.

Before this zone, many of Teodor / Scott’s choices return mostly to himself.

After this zone, every choice has another address.

A son is not a side path.

A son is not a cure.

A son is not proof that the father is worth saving.

A son is a person, a witness, a future, and a room the character can either help make safer or accidentally fill with unfinished weather.

This zone gives the final story generator material about fatherhood, love, distance, future responsibility, missed calls, repair, inheritance, and the terrifying realization that love alone does not make a person steady.

**Branch Group:** none  
**Opposite Space:** none

## Artifact

## Story Seed

Kaegan enters the board, and consequence changes. The character is no longer only surviving his own story. His choices now echo into someone else’s childhood, someone else’s memory, someone else’s understanding of what love feels like when it enters or leaves a room.

The final story should show fatherhood as responsibility, not redemption by default.

The final story may show him realizing too late that the child was witnessing more than he thought.

## Forced Consequence

A later choice returns through Kaegan’s eyes, and the character understands that consequence can inherit a face.

The final story should use this zone carefully.

The board must be able to hold all three.
