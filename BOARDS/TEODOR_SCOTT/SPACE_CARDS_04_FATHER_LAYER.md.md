# TEODOR / SCOTT SPACE CARDS

## Batch 04 — Father Layer

_Status: Board Space Cards | Spaces 25–32 | Father Layer Zone | AI Ending Fuel_

---

## Zone Function

The Father Layer zone marks the point where the board changes weight.

This zone is not about turning the father into a symbol.

The father must feel like a real person before the board is allowed to use him as a layer.

He is presence before absence.

He is humor before grief.

He is smell, voice, chair, timing, ordinary comments, stubborn love, and the strange safety a child can feel when one person makes the world survivable.

Then he is gone.

This zone gives the final story generator material about chosen fatherhood, grief, memory, smell, empty rooms, named loss, disguised loss, and the moment one death tilts the whole board.

# SPACE 25 — FATHER

**Zone:** Father Layer  
**Type:** Fixed Anchor  
**Reality Layer:** Family Layer / Memory Layer / Father Layer / Identity Layer  
**Branch Group:** none  
**Opposite Space:** none

## Glass Riddle

The man became real by staying.

## Artifact

Father’s Hand

## Story Seed

The father is not only the man who signed the papers or gave the name. He becomes real through repetition. He is there enough times that the child stops checking whether the door is still open. He is funny, stubborn, practical, flawed, present, and safe in the way real fathers can be safe without being perfect.

## Collect Meaning

The character accepts the father as a real anchor.

The final story should show the father as human, not mythic.

## Ignore Meaning

The character avoids looking directly at how much the father mattered.

The final story should show grief becoming harder to name because love was minimized first.

## Missed Meaning

The father remains present in the story, but the character does not understand the size of the anchor until after it is gone.

## Forced Consequence

A later room asks what held the character in place, and the answer returns wearing his father’s voice.

## Boolean Tags

```text
fixed_anchor = true
father_exists = true
father_as_real_person = true
father_anchor_weight += collected
father_importance_minimized += ignored
father_anchor_unrecognized += missed
father_voice_return += forced
```

## Ending Influence

This space must influence every Teodor / Scott ending.

If collected, the ending should show the father as a real man with texture and presence.

If ignored or missed, the ending should show the character discovering too late how much of his identity had been standing on that presence.
