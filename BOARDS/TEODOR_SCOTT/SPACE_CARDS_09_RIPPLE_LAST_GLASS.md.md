## Zone Function

The Ripple / Last Glass zone is where the pattern receives a name and then becomes responsibility.

This zone is not victory.

This zone is not proof that the character was right about everything.

This zone is where naming the pattern forces the character to answer for what the pattern does.

A ripple can heal.

A ripple can harm.

A ripple can become repair.

A ripple can become a wall.

A ripple can move through a father, into a son, into another son, and shape rooms before anyone involved has language for it.

This zone gives the final story generator material about naming, denial, consequence, accountability, wounds turned into architecture, monuments built from pain, and the final question the board asks at the Last Glass.
