## Zone Function

The AIFRED / INTERVENTION zone shows Teodor / Scott turning comparison, sound, memory, grief, and structure into tools.

AIFRED begins as a practical audio tool.

Not mythology.

Not theory.

A musician wants to compare a mix to a professional target and know what is different.

Tone. Stereo. Loudness. Dynamics. Width. Punch. Balance.

A simple problem.

But the character does not leave simple problems alone.

AIFRED becomes a diagnostic mirror.

Then INTERVENTION becomes the archive.

The place where grief, family, theory, fatherhood, artifacts, screenshots, chapters, public posts, private panic, and old rooms can be arranged instead of carried loose.

This zone gives the final story generator material about tools, mirrors, diagnosis, signal comparison, current state versus target state, writing, archiving, public performance, private repair, and the dangerous relief of finally having somewhere to put the mess.
