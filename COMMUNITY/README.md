# 🌀 Community Echoes

This folder is for reader echoes.

It is not the official story.

It is not the canon artifact layer.

It is the water answering.

---

## What Belongs Here?

Community echoes are reader-submitted fragments, field notes, reflections, or room reports inspired by INTERVENTION.

They may describe:

- a ripple someone noticed
- an intervention point
- a missed intervention point
- a loop becoming visible
- a room changing
- a signal traveling
- a door becoming possible
- feedback returning to the observer

Community echoes should be small, grounded, consent-based, and safe.

---

## Canon Boundary

The seventeen chapters are complete.

The official artifact layer lives in `INTERVENTION ARG/ARTIFACTS/`.

This folder is different.

A community echo is not canon unless the curator explicitly says it is.

Readers can add echoes.

Only the curator decides what becomes part of the room.

```text
ECHO: [short title]

STATUS: [noticed / traveling / interrupted / unresolved]

FIELD NOTE:
[short fragment, reflection, log, or recovered text]
```

## Rules

No private information.

No full names of people who did not consent.

No addresses, phone numbers, workplaces, or private messages.

No diagnosing strangers.

No targeting real people.

No harassment.

No conspiracy escalation.

No crisis posting as gameplay.

No story outranks the body.

No framework outranks consent.

No ripple excuses harm.
